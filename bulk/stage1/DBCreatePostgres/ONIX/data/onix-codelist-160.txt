01	Required credit	Credit that must be displayed when a resource is used (eg 'Photo Jerry Bauer' or '&copy; Magnum Photo'). Credit text should be carried in <FeatureNote>
02	Caption	explanatory caption that may accompany a resource (eg use to identify an author in a photograph). Caption text should be carried in <FeatureNote>
03	Copyright holder	copyright holder of resource (indicative only, as the resource can be used without consultation). Copyright text should be carried in <FeatureNote>
04	Length in minutes	approx length in minutes of an audio or video resource. <FeatureValue> should contain the length of time as an integer number of minutes
